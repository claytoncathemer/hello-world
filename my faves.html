Playing Guitar

I love playing guitar. At times of high stress, its my way of escaping for a brief moment to clear my head. I play classical style, which is basically fingerstyle except with very old traditions dating back to the 1600s. Not only is it very soothing to hear and play but, it can get very complex and exciting creating an awesome challenge for my fingers and brain to work on. Currently, I have 3 guitars in my collection:

  • my go-to Cordoba
  • my Schecter
  • my prized Gibson
